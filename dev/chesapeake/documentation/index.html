Welcome to the documentation for Chesapeake (tumblr). This documentation guide will walk you through the installation process for this theme, as well as several features and assets you should know about.

Installation

Installing this theme is fairly simple and requires no coding knowledge at all.

  1. Download your copy of Chesapeake from your themeforest dashboard.
  2. Unzip your download into a folder.
  3. Navigation to Tumblr and login.
  4. At the top right, click on the user icon and then "edit appearance" from the dropdown.
  5. Click "Edit HTML" at the top of the sidebar.
  6. In your download folder, right click "index.html" and open it in a text editor (do not open in it in a web browser).
  7. Select all the text in the file, copy it (ctrl+c), and then paste it in the tumblr "Edit HTML panel"
  8. At the top of the sidebar, select "Update Preview" and "Save".

There, all done! That wasn't so bad, right?

 

Customization

Chesapeake is customizable, making it easy to change the look of the theme to your liking. There are 10 color options and social media links as well as custom links and image feeds for certain social media outlets (Instagram, Flickr, and Dribbble) for your sidebar. Additionally, Chesapeake supports infinite scroll and disqus. Chesapeake also supports Disqus, just add your shortname in the "Disqus Shortname" field.

To customize your theme, once logged in to your tumblr dashboard, press the user icon at the top right. From the dropdown, select "Edit Appearance" and customize your blog.

  • Use the color picker to change the theme colors.
  • Turn on/off infinite scroll
  • Turn on/off showing social media links in the sidebar
  • 6 social media account links
  • Instagram ClientID/oAuth Token (click here to find out how to find these) for your Instagram feed on the sidebar
  • Flickr UserID (click here to find out how to find this) for your Flickr feed on the sidebar
  • Disqus Shortname to enable Disqus commenting system
  • 6 custom links for the sidebar
