Government Program Could Eliminate Electric Bills For Millions of Homeowners

by Solar Panel Activation.


A little known stimulus program that was passed in 2005 has been uncovered. The program is called the Energy Policy Act and it gives homeowners a once in a lifetime opportunity to get relief from their electric bills. This stimulus could help homeowners eliminate their electric bill and get a 30% tax credit simply by installing solar panels. President Obama is fighting to get this act extended, but as of now this credit will expire next year so its important.

Solar Program That Can Eliminate Electric Bills, Reduce Taxes , Help the Environment

According to the Washington Post, installing solar panels is on the ‘verge of no brainer status from a purely financial perspective.’ Homeowners can use solar panels to eliminate electric bills, to reduce their taxes, and to reduce their carbon footprint and help the environment. Qualified homeowners who’ve had solar panels installed could be saving up to $14,000 in their first year and could be on their way towards eliminating their next 25 years of electric payments.

Why Isn't Everyone Getting Solar Panels? Here's Why...

Power companies hate what solar panels and the government’s energy relief plan could do to them. In some cases these homeowners produce so much power that the energy companies actually end up having to pay them. The power companies hate this and they’ve been doing all they can to try and stop homeowners from taking advantage of solar power.
