Earned Income Tax Credit

American Opportunity Tax Credit
This credit is a replacement for the Hope Credit, and is intended to help cover some of the costs of higher education. [Do you qualify?]

Lifetime Learning Credit
The Lifetime Learning Credit also helps cover the cost of higher education, but it even includes people who are not going for a degree. [Do you qualify?]

Child and Dependent Care Credit
This credit is intended to help pay for the costs of daycare or babysitting for work related purposes, in addition to other forms of dependent care. [Do you qualify?]

Savers Tax Credit
The Savers Tax Credit can be granted for contributions to 401-K and other retirement programs. Allowing the taxpayer to both deduct an amount entirely, and claim a credit of up to $1,000. [Do you qualify?]
