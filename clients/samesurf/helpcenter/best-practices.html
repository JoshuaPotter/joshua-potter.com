Operating Systems

Samesurf supports desktop and mobile devices that run Windows, Mac OSX, Android and iOS.

Browsers

Samesurf supports virtually every major desktop and mobile browser including Internet Explorer (IE8+), Google Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Opera. For optimal results, we recommend using modern browsers while accessing the Samesurf suite of products.

Specific Screen Sharing Requirements

Unlike Samesurf Co-Browsing which carries no installs, screen sharing requires the leader to have Java installed on Windows computers and a one-time plug-in installation for Mac computers.

On Windows, leaders must have Java (version 1.6 or higher) installed on their computer to broadcast their screen to other participants.

On Mac, leaders must install a one-time plug-in for sharing their screen. There are no installs required to view screen sharing transmissions on receiving devices.

Audio & Video Chat

On supported browsers like Google Chrome and Opera, we leverage WebRTC technology to enable HD install-free audio and video chat.

Web Connectivity

Online collaboration tools like Samesurf exhibit better performance on higher end broadband connections like cable, DSL & 4G wireless. Users with download speeds of 5Mbps+ will enjoy more optimal experiences.

Screen Sharing Tips

Screen sharing may expose the leader's entire screen and is likely to require greater utilization levels for bandwidth, GPU and CPU than Co-Browsing. Here are some suggestions when using screen sharing:

Close extraneous windows prior to initiating screen sharing.

Confine screen sharing to a subset of your entire screen by adjusting the red box to suit your purposes.

Co-Browsing on Mobile

Samesurf co-browsing works seamlessly on mobile devices. However and if you answer a phone call during an active session, you may get disconnected from your session.

Perfectly Synced Videos

To enjoy perfectly synced videos that play, pause and skip together from the Youtube and Vimeo libraries, visit PublicRooms.com for open sessions and Samesurf Private Rooms for invite-only sessions.

Authenticated Sharing

Samesurf enables authenticated sharing on sites that require logins like Facebook and Dropbox. Even though only the leader will be able to interact with these sites during sessions, you should only pass leader control within these sites to people you trust and know well.

List In Public Rooms

The List in Public Rooms option makes active rooms visible within the Public Rooms directory so other users can easily find and join active sessions via a single click (coming soon).

If you're the leader within a public room, you should confine your co-browsing to sites that don't contain objectionable content or private information about you.
