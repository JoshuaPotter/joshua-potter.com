Start or Join a Room

Initiate a session by clicking the Start New Room button. You can join an existing room by triggering an invite link or typing the unique identifier into the Join a Room Box and then clicking Go.

Send Invites

Send out invites via the default email service on your computer, filling out the web form or copying and pasting the invite link. You will also soon be able to send invites to your online friends via Social Chat.

Sign In

Sign in by entering your name and then clicking Continue or use one of the integrated social networks to chat and send instant invites to your circle of online friends and contacts (coming soon).

Co-Browsing

Our install-free co-browsing technology serves as the core of our suite of offerings. The following features and methodologies come together to form the essence of patented co-browsing by Samesurf:

Synced Page Navigation

Samesurf empowers multiple people to browse together between and within pages without requiring installs, delays or coding of any kind.

Joint Form Filling and sensitive input field blocking

Participants have the option of filling out forms together by being able to simultaneously type within text input boxes. Field blocking of sensitive entries like passwords is also available for enterprise accounts.

Mouse Tracking

Users are able to track each other's cursors as they auto-scroll to follow the leader's movements within each page.

Screen Drawing

Participants can collaborate and guide one another by sharing screen markings on webpages and the interactive whiteboard (coming soon).

Security

Samesurf promotes security through built-in features like TLS/SSL encryption, limitation of content sharing to a single browser tab and input field blocking for sensitive text entries.

Authenticated Sharing

Our tools enable users to co-browse websites that require logins such as Facebook and Dropbox with people they trust and know well.

Text Chat

Text chat elevates the co-browsing experience by empowering users to instantly communicate reactions to what they're viewing via Samesurf and Social Chat.

Pass or Take Back Control

The pass control option enables either the host or current leader to designate a new person to lead the session. The take back control function enables the host to reclaim control at any time.

Request Control

The request control feature allows users to ask the current leader for control within co-browse sessions.

Share Videos

PublicRooms.com and Samesurf Private Rooms enable multiple people to watch perfectly synced videos from the Youtube and Vimeo libraries.

Switch Rooms

The switch rooms feature empowers active participants to exit existing sessions in order to start or join new ones.

List in Public Rooms

The List in Public Rooms option displays active sessions within our PublicRooms.com directory so users can easily identify and join active sessions (coming soon).

LogOut

The logout function enables users to exit active sessions. An even simpler way to leave active sessions is to close the current co-browse tab.

Help

The help function displays a simple road map for the primary features. If additional information is desired, this Help Center will provide further insight into the inner workings of Samesurf.

Screen Sharing

Activate screen sharing to broadcast applications and offline content that reside on your computer's desktop. You can either share your entire screen or confine sharing to the portion within the red box.

Samesurf enables screen broadcasts into mobile devices
without requiring downloads or installs of any kind.

Audio & Video Chat

Enhance the shared experience via built-in Audio and Video Chat solutions that leverage webRTC on supported browsers like Google Chrome and Opera.

Samesurf & Social Chat

Elevate your co-browsing experience by communicating reactions to shared content via text chat. Send messages to other participants or chat and transmit Samesurf invites across your social networks (coming soon).

Upload & Share

Upload images and presentations to the Samesurf cloud and guide other participants through crystal clear pictures and slides from and into virtually any device (coming soon).

Analytics

Garner valuable insights on site usage to help develop products and campaigns that better reflect your users' patterns and preferences.

Sameslide

Sameslide enables multiple users to deliver and enjoy install-free presentations from virtually any device. It fuses slide decks in the cloud with crystal clear co-browsing and perfectly synced video sharing to simulate the experience of being together in real life.

Step 1 - Create

Start by uploading or selecting the slides, videos and websites you'll use to craft and deliver your presentation. An uploaded PDF will be instantly converted into a series of co-browse ready slides.

Step 2 - Organize

Organize and select the order in which your slides, videos and websites will appear during your presentation.

Step 3 - Preview

Preview the various components of your presentation before sending invitations to other participants.

Step 4 - Confirm

A confirmation is sent and you have the option to schedule a presentation now or in the future.

Step 5 - Deliver

Deliver install-free presentations from virtually any device or browser with the instant option of toggling into co-browsing or screen sharing.
