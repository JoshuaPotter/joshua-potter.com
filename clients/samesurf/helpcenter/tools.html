Features

Co-Browsing

Browse the web together in real-time from virtually any device or browser.

Control Passing

Request and pass leader controls to take turns co-navigating through online and offline content.

Mouse Tracking

Track each other's cursors while you auto-scroll to match the current leader's movements on each page.

Screen Drawing

Collaborate and guide one another by sharing screen markings on webpages and the interactive whiteboard (coming soon).

Audio & Video Chat

Enhance your experience through built-in audio and video chat featuring webRTC.

Social Integration

Exchange social messages, posts and invites from a single place as you browse the web together (coming soon).

Screen Sharing

Activate screen sharing to broadcast apps and offline content from your desktop.

Upload & Share

Upload presentations to the cloud and guide participants through each slide without installs from any device (coming soon).

Security

Safeguard data via TLS/SSL encryption, field blocking and confining co-browsing to a single browser tab.

Authenticated Sharing

Co-browse sites that require logins such as Facebook and Dropbox with people you trust and know well.

Analytics

Gain valuable insights on site usage to develop campaigns that better reflect your users' patterns and preferences.

Text Chat

Elevate the co-browsing experience by instantly communicating reactions to shared content via chat.
