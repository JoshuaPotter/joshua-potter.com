For Websites

Click to Start

A publisher who integrates Samesurf functionality onto their website has the option to place a Start Button anywhere on that site. You can instantly start or join an existing room by clcking the Start Button.

Click the Browse Together Button to Start the Session.

Start or Join a Room

You can initiate a session by clicking the Start New Room button. You can join an existing room by triggering an invite link or typing the unique identifier into the Join a Room Box and then clicking Go.

Click the Start New Room Button to Trigger the Session.

Sign In

You can now enter your name and click the Continue button to enter the session or login using a social network service where you can chat and send instant invites to your circle of online friends and contacts.

Change your Name (optional) and click Continue.

Send Invites

You are now able to send out invites via the default email service on your computer or mobile device, filling out the web form or copying and pasting the invite link into an IM client.

Click the Invite button and Send Invites.

Browse Together

You can now lead co-browsing from page to page, pass the leader controls and share the infinite beauty of the web in real-time. You're able to navigate by entering full addresses into the URL Entry Bar.

The Leader Navigates via the Samesurf URL Entry Bar.
